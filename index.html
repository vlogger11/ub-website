Team

The Leadership

Three generations. One family. One standard. The leadership team ensuring the United vision remains untarnished.

01

Mr. Rajan Daryani

Partner, United Builders

An engineer by education (B.E. Computer, Bangalore) with an MBA in Finance, Mr. Rajan Daryani leads United Builders with a rare blend of technical precision and management acumen.

For over four decades, he has meticulously driven residential, commercial, and plotted developments across Nashik. Renowned for his financial discipline and methodical planning, his leadership ensures that every project balances thoughtful design, functionality, and long-term value, cementing his role in Nashik’s real estate legacy.

02

Mr. Pramod Daryani

Partner, United Builders

A Civil Engineer by education, Mr. Pramod Daryani is a key second-generation leader who strengthens the organization's execution capabilities. He combines a strong technical foundation with a progressive mindset.

He is constantly exploring contemporary design and future-ready development practices. With a hands-on approach to operations, he maintains rigorous control over project timelines and delivery frameworks, ensuring execution excellence established by the founding leadership.

03

Mr. Karan Daryani

Partner, United Builders

Representing the third generation, Mr. Karan Daryani brings a global perspective. He holds a degree in Architecture Engineering & Design Management (UK) and a Masters in Project & Enterprise Management (UCL).

Blending design thinking with structured execution, he is passionate about creating lifestyle-centric communities. By introducing innovative formats, Karan ensures every square foot is responsibly developed, future-ready, and meticulously planned.

04

Mrs. Riya Daryani

Marketing Head, United Builders

Educated in Business Administration across India and New York, Mrs. Riya Daryani is the decisive force behind the brand’s evolution. She has shifted the perception of the firm toward a design-forward luxury brand.

Beyond marketing, Riya plays a formative role in interior design and styling. She ensures an uncompromising coherence between the brand's global aesthetic and the physical living spaces, oversee-ing every finish to deliver a vision that truly reflects the brand.
